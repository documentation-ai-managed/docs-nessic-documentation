## School Onboarding

Schools can seamlessly integrate with NESSIC to enhance collaboration and streamline operations. The platform allows institutions to connect with other schools, share resources, and manage student data efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register Institution" icon="building">
    Create an account for your school and verify your institutional credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings" icon="settings">
    Set up your school's profile, including contact information and preferred collaboration options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Partners" icon="users">
    Link with other schools and educational partners to begin sharing resources.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Data" icon="upload">
    Upload student and staff data to populate your NESSIC dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

To maximize the benefits of NESSIC, schools should follow these guidelines.

| Practice | Description | Benefit |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Regular Updates | Keep student and staff data current | Accurate reporting |
